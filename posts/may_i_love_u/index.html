Notice me, focus on me, look at me.
I always talk to myself about the following question: How should I proceed to become a successful man like my father or my grandfather? But I have never arrived at a final answer. I can’t even emulate them when they were as young as I am now. Therefore, I promise myself that I will do my utmost to become a better man than they were.
I am captivated by the beauty of the world, and I draw strength from it. I find it fascinating to study philosophy, mathematics (especially algorithms), foreign languages, coding, and more. These hobbies help me to understand the world more clearly. I hope I can continue on this path.
